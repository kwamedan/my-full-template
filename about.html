About Gym For You

Overview

Gym For You was created in 1947 by brothers Dave and Nigel Cumberman.
They believed that fitness and physical health were the way to help relieve tension and depression in a world that had been destroyed by the world war.
Starting in-front of their doorway, they invited friends over to access weights they had brought back from France whilst at war. Soon enough, they had the whole neighbourhood involved and decided to branch into a larger area to accomodate more clients.
In June 1948, they bought out their own mini-stall along the street which attracted more customers and helped generate enough income to hire their 1st two employees to help the business grow.
Now, The Gym Group is one of the largest and most popular in the UK attracting over 2,000 users a day who try us for a variety of reasons whether that's for peace of mind, for getting fit or creating a better body image for themselves.
On average, we have 900 users enter our gym per day but don't worry because this doesn't halt us as there's plenty of duplicate equipment for many people to work the same exercise.
We also boast the UK's 1st gender separate section, where female members can decide to work in a space separate from the male gender.
